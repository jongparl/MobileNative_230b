Multimedia: Handling Audio, Video, Camera, and Images

Tizen multimedia features enable your application to capture and play or display still images, video, and sounds. Multimedia features are provided by the Multimedia API, which enables you to integrate audio, video, image processing, and streaming media into your applications.

The Media module provides the following main features:

  • Playing media data
    • Playing audio, Playing WAV, and playing PCM audio

      Create a multimedia application to play compressed audio data or uncompressed PCM data.

    • Playing video

      Enable your application to play compressed video data in various multimedia files.

    • Playing tones

      Enable your application to play a tone or list of tones.

    • Media player states

      Manage the state of the media player.

  • Capturing and recording media data
    • Camera

      Launch the camera and control or adjust camera sensors of the device to preview or capture still images.

    • Recording audio

      Record audio data to a file with compressed or uncompressed format or capture uncompressed PCM data from audio device.

    • Recording video

      Generate compressed video files with video data from camera and audio data from audio input device.

  • Image Util

    View and process (decode, encode, convert, and compress) bitmap images in the JPEG format.

  • Video Util

    Use video transcoding features, such as multi-format codec and output format.

  • Metadata Extractor

    Extract metadata from the media files.
